Welcome

Little Flower School is committed to providing quality education, strong moral values, discipline, and holistic development of children. We strive to prepare students for a responsible and successful future.

About the School

Little Flower School is situated in Hidim Teron Village, Manja, Karbi Anglong District of Assam. The school focuses on academic excellence, character formation, and co-curricular development in a safe environment.

Principal’s Message

“Education is not merely learning facts, but training the mind and heart. At Little Flower School, we emphasize discipline, values, and knowledge to shape responsible citizens of tomorrow.”
